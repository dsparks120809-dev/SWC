bio

SWC (Slit Wrist County / $uicide County) is an underground rap duo from South Carolina. We’re not here to change the world — just to show a different perspective through raw sound, emotion, and energy. Built on chaos, honesty, and underground culture, SWC represents a mindset more than a movement.
